QuikSplit

Splitting bills can be a pain. How about an app that scans receipts and lets users pick their dishes, then handles tax, tip, and payments?

The QuikSplit app will be perfect for eating out with friends. To split a bill, simply:

1. Snap a picture of your receipt
2. Match each item to its purchaser
3. Let QuikSplit handle all your Venmo requests
